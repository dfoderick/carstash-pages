Privacy Policy
At Carstash Limited, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. We understand that your personal information is important and we take the responsibility of safeguarding it very seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it and the steps we take to protect it.
We collect personal information such as your name, address, email address, and telephone number when you place an order or create an account with us. This information is used to process and fulfill your orders, and to communicate with you about your order. We may also use your personal information to send you promotional emails or to contact you with special offers.
We use appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. We also use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt your personal information during transmission to our servers.
We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except as necessary to process and fulfill your order or as required by law.
We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time, so please review it frequently. Your continued use of our website and services will signify your acceptance of any changes to this Policy.
If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@carstash.io.
